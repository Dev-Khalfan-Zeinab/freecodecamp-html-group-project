Reading books has various advantages and disadvantages as explained in this Books App.

The choice of reading a book can be categorised into three:

  • Advantages
  • Disadvantages
  • Types of Books

Advantages of Reading Books

  1. Brain health and cognitive development
  2. Mental well-being and stress reduction
  3. Knowledge and perspective
  4. Improved communication skills
  5. Enhanced imagination and creativity

Disadvantages of Too Much Reading

  1. Physical strain
  2. Sedentary behaviour
  3. Reduced original thinking
  4. Information overload
  5. Neglect of duties
